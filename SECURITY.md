Supported Versions
CrossNordics provides security updates only for actively maintained versions of our internal tooling and operational infrastructure. Older versions may continue to function but do not receive security patches or backported fixes.

Reporting a Vulnerability
We strongly encourage responsible disclosure and appreciate the efforts of security researchers who help improve the safety and reliability of our systems.

If you believe you have discovered a security vulnerability affecting any CrossNordics service, infrastructure component, or documentation, please follow the process below.

How to Report
Send an email to:

security@crossnordics.com

Please include:

A clear and concise description of the issue

Steps to reproduce the vulnerability

Any proof‑of‑concept material (screenshots, payloads, logs, etc.)

The potential impact if known

Your preferred method of contact

We ask that you do not publicly disclose the vulnerability before we have had a chance to investigate and address it.

What to Expect
Once your report is submitted:

You will receive an acknowledgment within 72 hours

Our security team will review the report and begin an internal assessment

We may contact you for additional information

If the issue is confirmed, we will work to remediate it as quickly as possible

We will notify you once the fix has been deployed or if the report is declined

We aim to maintain transparency throughout the process.

Responsible Disclosure Guidelines
To ensure a safe and ethical testing environment, we ask that researchers:

Avoid accessing unnecessary data

Do not disrupt services or degrade performance

Do not attempt social engineering against CrossNordics personnel

Do not use automated scanning tools that may impact service stability

Refrain from exploiting the vulnerability beyond what is necessary to demonstrate it

Following these guidelines helps protect both our systems and our users.

Recognition
While CrossNordics does not currently operate a public bug bounty program, we may privately acknowledge significant contributions at our discretion.

Security Commitment
CrossNordics is committed to:

Maintaining a secure operational environment

Applying security patches promptly

Conducting regular internal audits and reviews

Following Nordic best practices for digital infrastructure

Ensuring transparency and accountability in our security processes
